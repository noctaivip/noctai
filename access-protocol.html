ACCESS GOVERNANCE FRAMEWORK

Access Protocol — NCT-AP-001

NOCTAI operates as a private strategic investment intelligence layer. Access to information, structures, and opportunities is governed by a controlled disclosure protocol designed to protect structural integrity, capital priority, and jurisdictional positioning.

Purpose of the Protocol

This protocol defines how access to dossiers is evaluated, granted, limited, or withdrawn. It exists to ensure that disclosure occurs only when strategic alignment, capital qualification, and timing conditions are satisfied.

Key Definitions

Disclosure Window — A temporary period during which limited information may be shared.

Capital Stage — The current phase of structural capital positioning (pre-allocation, reservation, deployment).

Reservation — A temporary priority hold granted to a qualified party.

Rotation — The removal or reclassification of a dossier based on timing or structure changes.

Access Sequence

  1. Identity and capital qualification review
  2. NDA framework acknowledgement
  3. Selective disclosure of non-sensitive materials
  4. Participation confirmation or decline
  5. Dossier proceeds, rotates, or closes
