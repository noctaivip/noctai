PROJECT ACCESS

Access to platform projects

Project Access is a customized level of access to specific investment projects and closed rounds on the NoctAI platform. The decision to grant access is made manually and is based on the investor's total participation.

Public projects and their current statuses are presented in the Projects section.

Go to Projects →

Conditions of consideration

Requests for Project Access are considered with the combined participation of от $10,000 in platform projects.

  • the total investment volume is taken into account, not one tranche
  • access is not granted automatically
  • The platform reserves the right to refuse without disclosing the reasons.

What does Project Access provide

  • access to closed and future projects
  • early access to individual investment rounds
  • private updates and materials on projects

Request for Project Access

To review your request, please provide a contact email and Transaction Hash (TxID) confirming your participation in the platform's projects.

All requests are reviewed manually. If approved, access is granted via the email address provided.

Project Access does not guarantee participation in closed projects. All investment decisions are made individually.
