Oversight & Governance

NoctAI Investment Platform Responsibility and Governance Framework

Control principle

The NoctAI platform is managed by a centralized investment team, which is fully responsible for project selection, legal structure, patent protection, and investor admission.

The platform is not a marketplace and does not use automated investment access mechanisms.

Decision-making chain

Expertise → Legal Review → Patent/Protection → Structuring → Opening Access

No stage can be skipped. Decisions are made manually and recorded within the management team.

Investment access control

Investment access is not a public right and is granted selectively.

Access may be restricted or revoked in case of non-compliance with the platform terms, changes in the project structure, or regulatory factors.

Based on this governance model, the Access Protocol is being developed—a formal procedure for access, disclosure, and recording of investor participation. Go to Access Protocol →

Absence of public figures

NoctAI is an infrastructure investment platform, not a personal brand or media project.

Responsibility is fixed at the level of the management system, procedures and decisions, and not at the level of public figures.

Limitations and boundaries of management

The platform operates under an internal investment mandate that defines the types of projects allowed, risk levels and capital admission structure.

No project can be opened for investment outside of an approved model of responsibility and protection of investors' interests.

This page outlines the platform's responsibility and governance architecture. All investment conditions are determined individually.
