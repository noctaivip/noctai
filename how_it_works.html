HOW IT WORKS

How NoctAI's participation model works

NoctAI is a platform for equity participation in real infrastructure projects. Participation is recorded through manual verification, and investor shares are secured within a single ownership structure.

Why does the minimum entry start at $1?

The minimum entry is not a promise of profitability and is not intended for speculative expectations.

Unified participation structure

The minimum threshold allows for the formation of a single investor pool, in which all participants are part of the same ownership structure, regardless of the size of the deposit.

Model scalability

A low entry threshold is used to scale the project. and distribution of shares, and not to create the illusion of quick results.

Proportional fixation

Each participation is fixed in proportion to the invested capital. and is subject to uniform distribution rules.

How does participation work with small capital

Share in the common pool

The participant receives a share in the general investment pool, the size of which is determined by the contribution relative to the total volume of capital.

Dependence on project development

The potential for participation depends on the development of the project, its operational performance and scaling.

No guarantees

Participation does not imply a guaranteed return. and is associated with investment risks.

Why are settlements conducted in USDT

Unified calculation standard

Using USDT provides a uniform and predictable calculation standard for all participants.

Transparency and verification

All transactions are verifiable and simplify share accounting.

Preparing for scaling

This model simplifies subsequent connection. additional calculation mechanisms.

STEP 1

Selecting a project

You select an active project on the platform and become familiar with the investment round parameters.

STEP 2

Sending USDT

Investments are accepted in USDT (TRC20). After sending funds, you'll be taken to confirmation.

STEP 3

Transaction confirmation

You provide a contact email and Transaction Hash (TxID). This allows you to secure your investment.

STEP 4

Ручная проверка

The NoctAI team manually confirms the transaction and locks in the investor's stake.

Почему используется ручная проверка

Manual verification reduces the risk of errors, protects investors, and maintains control over the share structure.

Timeframe for fixing the share

Confirmation and fixation of the share occurs within 24 hours after sending the data.

Large investments

Investments from major investors are considered individually through direct contact with the team.

NoctAI is not a public investment offer. All investments are made based on the individual decision of the investor.
