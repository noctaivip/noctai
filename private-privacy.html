PRIVACY & CONFIDENTIALITY NOTICE

Privacy

NOCTAI is designed to operate with minimal data exposure. Privacy, discretion, and confidentiality are treated as structural requirements, not compliance checkboxes.

1. Information Collection

NOCTAI does not operate as a consumer platform and does not collect personal data for marketing, profiling, or resale.

Limited information may be provided voluntarily through controlled access signals, secure communications, or bilateral correspondence.

2. Use of Information

Any information provided to NOCTAI is used exclusively for:

– assessing strategic alignment
– evaluating access eligibility
– administering controlled disclosure

Information is never used for advertising, analytics resale, or behavioral tracking.

3. Data Minimization

NOCTAI intentionally limits data retention to what is operationally necessary. Excess or irrelevant information is neither requested nor stored.

There is no obligation to submit personal identifiers, financial statements, or documentation at the access request stage.

4. Confidentiality & Security

Information received by NOCTAI is treated as confidential unless explicitly stated otherwise.

Access to information is restricted internally on a need-to-know basis and may be further governed by contractual confidentiality instruments.

5. Third Parties

NOCTAI does not sell, rent, or disclose information to third parties for commercial purposes.

Limited disclosure may occur only where required by law or necessary to protect the legal integrity of NOCTAI.

6. Jurisdictional Considerations

NOCTAI operates across multiple jurisdictions. Information handling practices are aligned with applicable legal frameworks where relevant.

This notice is not intended to create consumer privacy rights or statutory entitlements beyond applicable law.

7. Updates

This Privacy Notice may be updated periodically. Continued interaction with NOCTAI constitutes acceptance of the current version.
